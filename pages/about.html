About Me

I am a curious, empathy-driven UX Designer. Through my diverse roles, from construction worker to professional violist, I’ve had the opportunity to observe the way people operate. Learning about their interests, daily habits, and pain points as humans navigating life has fueled my passion for design.

My goal is to make lives easier through inclusive design.

In my free time, I enjoy poking around in the woods, riding my bicycle, and figuring out what makes people tick.
